Develop a Smart Healthcare Appointment System where patients book appointments and doctors
manage schedules.
You must implement Frontend + Backend using Microservices and Event-Driven Architecture
with proper security.
Requirements
• Frontend: Next.js (protected dashboards for doctor & patient)
• Backend: Minimum 3 microservices
o   Auth/User Service
    Appointment Service
    Notification Service (triggered by events)
• Event Driven:
    Booking/approval must emit events and Notification Service must respond.
• Security:
     HttpOnly cookies, protected routes with middleware, no secrets on frontend.
• Database: SQL or NoSQL (your choice).
• Deployment: Must be deployed (Vercel).
